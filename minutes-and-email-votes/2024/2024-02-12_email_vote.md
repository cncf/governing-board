# CNCF Governing Board Action Taken by Electronic Voting

February 12, 2024

The Falco project requested an exception (See [Issue \#629](https://github.com/cncf/foundation/issues/629)) to the CNCF IP Policy for use of the following libraries:

1. [**libcurl**](https://github.com/curl/curl), under the [curl](https://spdx.org/licenses/curl.html) license  
2. [**uthash**](https://github.com/troydhanson/uthash), under the [BSD-1-Clause](https://spdx.org/licenses/BSD-1-Clause.html) license  
3. [**libelf**](https://sourceware.org/elfutils/) (component of elfutils), under the [LGPL-3.0-or-later](https://www.gnu.org/licenses/lgpl-3.0.en.html) license, with some header files under [LGPL-2.1-or-later](https://www.gnu.org/licenses/old-licenses/lgpl-2.1.en.html)*.*

On January 30, 2024, the CNCF Legal Committee (LC) recommended that the Governing Board:

1. APPROVE the requested license exception for libcurl  
2. APPROVE the requested license exception for uthash  
3. APPROVE the requested license exception for libelf ***for dynamic linking only*** (but not for static linking at this time)

During a voting period that commended on January 31, 2024 and closed on February 12, 2024, the CNCF Governing Board voted by email to:

1. APPROVE the requested license exception for libcurl  
2. APPROVE the requested license exception for uthash  
3. APPROVE the requested license exception for libelf ***for dynamic linking only*** (but not for static linking at this time)
