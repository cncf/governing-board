# RESOLUTIONS OF THE CNCF GOVERNING BOARD

Approved by email vote on May 28, 2025

## **Amendments to CNCF Charter**

WHEREAS, to enable development of cloud native specifications and standards, CNCF intends to establish a sister entity in the form of a Series of Joint Development Foundation Projects, LLC.

WHEREAS, the Legal Committee recommends approval of the proposed amendments to the CNCF Charter attached as Exhibit A to specify the license terms that will apply to specifications and standards developed within CNCF or its sister entity.

RESOLVED, that the amendments to the CNCF Charter attached as Exhibit A are hereby approved and adopted.

## **License Exception Request for gRPC-Rust**

WHEREAS, several CNCF projects have requested exceptions to the CNCF IP Policy, which are subject to approval by the Governing Board under Section 11(g) of the CNCF Charter.

WHEREAS, the non-CNCF project Hyperium would like to transfer its **tonic** repository ([https://github.com/hyperium/tonic](https://github.com/hyperium/tonic)), which is licensed under the MIT license, to gRPC. The gRPC project maintainers would like to accept this transfer to [gRPC’s GitHub org](http://github.com/grpc), rename it “grpc-rust”, and continue future developments of this tooling under the “grpc” name while also continuing to maintain the existing “tonic” project as a continuing branch in the same repository.

WHEREAS, the gRPC project maintainers have requested approval of a license exception (Issue \#[985](https://github.com/cncf/foundation/issues/985)) to enable the grpc-rust repository to be licensed under one of several options.

WHEREAS, the CNCF Legal Committee recommends APPROVAL of an exception to allow the tonic/grpc-rust repository to continue to be licensed under the MIT License (and only the MIT License), and for future contributions to that repository to be made under the MIT License rather than CNCF’s standard Apache-2.0 license.

RESOLVED, that the Governing Board hereby APPROVES an exception to allow the tonic/grpc-rust repository to continue to be licensed under the MIT License for existing as well as future contributions.

## **License Exception Request for MySql.Data build dependency for OpenTelemetry**

WHEREAS, the OpenTelemetry project is requesting a license exception (Issue \#[995](https://github.com/cncf/foundation/issues/995)) for the [MySql.Data component](https://www.nuget.org/packages/MySql.Data), which is licensed under GPL-2.0-only WITH the [Universal FOSS Exception v1.0](https://spdx.org/licenses/Universal-FOSS-exception-1.0.html), to enable use of the MySql.Data connector as part of build and test tooling.

WHEREAS, the CNCF Legal Committee recommends APPROVAL of the requested exception.

RESOLVED, that the Governing Board hereby APPROVES an exception to allow use of the [MySql.Data component](https://www.nuget.org/packages/MySql.Data) by the OpenTelemetry project as described in its request.

## **License Exception Request for Neo4j dependency for Cartography**

WHEREAS, the Cartography project is requesting a license exception (Issue \#[58](https://github.com/cncf/sandbox/issues/58)) for use of the Neo4j graph database, which is licensed under GPL-3.0, as a mandatory dependency.

WHEREAS, the CNCF Legal Committee recommends REJECTION of the requested exception.

RESOLVED, that the Governing Board hereby REJECTS the Cartography project’s request for an exception to allow its use of the NEo4j graph database.
