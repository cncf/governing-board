# **CNCF Governing Board (GB)**

Welcome to the official repository for the **Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF) Governing Board**.

## **Governance and Purpose**

The CNCF Governing Board operates under the [CNCF Charter](https://github.com/cncf/foundation/blob/main/charter.md). The board’s primary responsibilities include:

* Establishing and managing the annual budget.  
* Defining marketing and ecosystem strategy.  
* Overseeing legal, trademark, and compliance matters.

For technical oversight of CNCF projects, please refer to the [Technical Oversight Committee (TOC)](https://github.com/cncf/toc). For end user concerns, see the [Technical Advisory Board (TAB)](https://github.com/cncf/tab).

## **Board Members**

The Governing Board is composed of:

* Appointed representatives of Platinum Members  
* Elected representatives of Gold and Silver Members  
* Elected representatives from CNCF projects  
* Chair of the [Technical Oversight Committee](https://github.com/cncf/toc)  
* Chair of the [End User Technical Advisory Board](https://github.com/cncf/tab)

Rather than maintaining a list in this repository, the official and most up-to-date roster of members can be found here:

👉 [**CNCF Governing Board Members**](https://www.cncf.io/people/governing-board/)

## **Minutes and Email Votes**

The CNCF Governing Board began publishing minutes of its meetings in 2020\. All minutes and results of email voting are available in this repository.

## **Communication**

* **Private Mailing List**  
* **Slack:** Private channel #gb on [CNCF Slack](https://slack.cncf.io/)  
* **Meetings:** The Governing Board meets three times per year.   
* **Minutes and Votes:** Meeting minutes and email votes are archived in this repository.

## **Governance and Policies**

The Governing Board’s activities are subject to the following policies:

* [Linux Foundation Antitrust Policy](https://www.linuxfoundation.org/legal/antitrust-policy)  
* [CNCF Code of Conduct](https://github.com/cncf/foundation/blob/main/code-of-conduct.md)  
* [CNCF Political Neutrality Policy](https://www.cncf.io/political-neutrality-policy/)

The Governing Board’s internal operational procedures are described in the [CNCF Charter](https://github.com/cncf/foundation/blob/main/charter.md) and the documents contained in [Governance and Operations](https://github.com/cncf/governing-board/tree/main/governance-and-operations).
