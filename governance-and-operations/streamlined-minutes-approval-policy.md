# CNCF Governing Board

# Meeting Minutes Approval Process

This approval process is intended to facilitate streamlined timely review and approval of CNCF Governing Board (GB) meeting minutes.

**1\.	Circulation of Draft Minutes.** Approximately 1-2 weeks after each CNCF GB meeting, CNCF staff will circulate draft minutes from the meeting to the GB by email, asking GB members to respond with any requested changes. 

**2\.	Review Period.** GB members shall have an initial “Review Period” of at least 3 weeks to review the draft minutes and propose changes. The email circulating the draft minutes shall specify the last day of the Review Period.

**3\.	Absence of Changes.** If no changes are proposed during the Review Period, the minutes shall be deemed approved.

**4\.	Changes Proposed.** If any changes are proposed during the initial Review Period, the Review Period will automatically be extended by 1 week, and GB members may object to such changes within the extended Review Period.

**(a)	 Absence of Sustained Objections.** If there are no objections to a proposed change, or if all objections to the change have been withdrawn or otherwise resolved, the change will be incorporated into the minutes, and the revised minutes including such change will be deemed approved.

**(b) 	Resolution of Sustained Objections.** If any objections raised during the extended Review Period are not withdrawn or otherwise resolved, whether the requested change will be incorporated into the minutes will be determined in accordance with the Governing Board’s standard decision-making procedures.

**Approval History**
